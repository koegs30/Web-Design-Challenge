Wind Speed


The chart above plots wind speeds and latitude for 500+ randomly selected cities. It would appear that, generally, it is not very common for cities to have windspeeds over 30. Most recorded windspeeds appear to be below 15. The limited data below -60 degrees latitude is probably a reflection of the lack of monitoring stations, not a lack of measurable wind.
